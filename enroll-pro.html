  • Enrollment Procedures

Welcome! We are thrilled that you have taken interest in studying at St. John Learning Center, Inc. We encourage you to explore our program offerings and discover a one of a kind learning experience with SJELCI.

FOR NEW STUDENTS


Step 1

  • Secure a priority number and/or registration form at the designated tables (make sure to fill-in all necessary data e.g. name, date of birth, contact number, modality of learning)
  • Prepare a long brown long envelope containing the following:
    • Report Card/Form 138
Step 2

  • Proceed to the designated waiting area and wait for your turn to be accommodated
  • Present your long brown envelope containing the accomplished enrollment form and other pertinent documents to the cashier/registrar’s office for checking.
Step 3

  • Pay the required minimum registration fee

FOR OLD AND RETURNING STUDENTS


Step 1

  • Secure a priority number and/or registration form at the designated tables (make sure to fill-in all necessary data e.g. name, date of birth, contact number, modality of learning)
  • Prepare a long brown long envelope containing the following:
    • Report Card/Form 138
Step 2

  • Proceed to the designated waiting area and wait for your turn to be accommodated
  • Present your long brown envelope containing the accomplished enrollment form and other pertinent documents to the cashier/registrar’s office for checking.
Step 3

  • Pay the required minimum registration fee

FOR TRANSFEREES


Step 1

  • Your first step is to fill out our registration form. The Enrollment Form will get you set up in our system and get your admission process started.
  • Prepare a long brown long envelope containing the following:
    • Enrollment form
    • Photocopy of PSA/NSO Birth Certificate
    • Report Card/Form 138
    • Certificate of Good Moral Character
Step 2

  • Present your long brown envelope containing the accomplished enrollment form and other pertinent documents to the cashier/registrar’s office for checking.
  • Pay the registration fee at the cashier’s office.
