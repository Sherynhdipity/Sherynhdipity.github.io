  • What St. John Is

St. John Early Learning Center, Inc. has been one of the leading educational institutions for the past years. With its intense desire to cater to the more challenging needs of every individual, it always make a step to make education better every year. It is very optimistic that people who believe in its dreams will embrace the new and lofty ideals.

THE DEVELOPMENT

St. John Early Learning Center, Inc. has started from a dream of giving proper education to children of families with average and below average income particularly to the residents of Tacurong City and nearby towns in the province of Sultan Kudarat.

It was during the summer of 1985 when SJELCI twenty-three (23) pupils opened its tutorial and remedial classes to pupils coming from the different schools in Tacurong City who ranged from ages three (3) to six (6) years old and were considered as shy and slow learners in their schools. With God's grace and the school's educational assistance program, these pupils, upon their return to their respective schools, were able to perform better in their students. The thankful parents then encouraged Mrs. Rosalinda C. Fresco, the Founder, to open pre-school classes.

The school strategically located 200 meters away from the National Highway, was officially open in the year 1987 with the duly approved permit No. 0055, to operate pre-school courses: Nursery, Kinder 1, and Prep. A year later was the opening of the Elementary with the permit No. 0056 issued by the Regional Director, DECSRO, Cotabato City and after 6 years of operation it gained government recognition through the Department of Education, Culture, and Sports with GR No. 015 series of 1993.

With the growing number of enrollees, SJELCI is now with an area of more than 6,000 square meters including the four (4) annex areas moved on from Preschool, Gradeshool, to Junior High School in which the opening of the Preschool was in 1987, the Elementary in 1989, and the Junior High School in 1997. The three (3) departments gained the latest government recognition issued by the Department of Education Region XII dated January 2, 2014 with the Recognition No. TC500501-008 series of 2014 (Preschool), TC500501-006 series of 2014 (Elementary), TC500501-0008 series of 2014 (Junior High School). School Year 2016 - 2017, the school was given a provisional permit to operate Senior High School with a Permit No. SHS-R12-144 series of 2016 offering Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS), and General Academic Strand (GAS) Strands.

SJELCI is a non-sectarian school and is committed to giving each student the values he/she needs to live a life based on truth, freedom, and love. SJELCI is now what it is today because of its commitment to serve the best way it can in the field of education.
